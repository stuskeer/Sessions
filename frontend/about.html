Welcome to Kitesurf Session Tracker

A web-based application designed for tracking and analyzing your kitesurfing sessions. Built for personal use with no login required - all your data is stored locally in a SQLite database.

2. Logging Sessions

On the Sessions page (main page), click "â• Add Session" to expand the form:

  • Required fields: Location, Date, Kite, Board, Session Type, Duration (HH:MM)
  • Optional fields: Max jump height, Wind speed, Wind direction, Max speed, Max jump distance, Max airtime

Click "Add Session" to save. Your new session will appear in the table below.

3. Managing Sessions

Click "Fetch Sessions" to load all your sessions into the table. You can:

  • Filter: Type in the filter box to search across all columns
  • Sort: Click column headers to sort by date, duration, wind speed, jump height, etc.
  • Edit: Click the "Edit" button on any session to modify details
  • Delete: Click the "Delete" button to remove a session (with confirmation)
